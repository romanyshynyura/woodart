Placeholder

nullby default

If you feel like adding a placeholder, that's displayed before anything is selected. Once a selection has been made, the placeholder will be removed until the user clicks the SoD again. Then it will be shown until the user makes a new selection or focus is lost on the sod. The "placeholder" is given the class.
